About

The Four Elms Inn

The Four Elms Inn is a traditional village pub situated in Four Elms Village near Edenbridge, Kent. We are close to many popular tourist attractions including Hever Castle and Chartwell. The surrounding area is also great for walking and cycling and so we are pleased to welcome many visitors each year as well as our faithful locals and regulars.

We are housed in a wonderful old listed building with parts dating from the 16th century although it has only been a pub since the 1880s. We have a public bar with inglenook fireplace..

Free House

A "Free House" is basically a pub that doesn't limit itself to selling only one brewery's offerings. If you want to warm up the old-fashioned way, the drinks menu offers an ample variety of whiskeys and mixed drinks.

Themed Evenings

The Four Elms Inn

Our highly acclaimed Themed Food Evenings are held every month. Themes vary considerably, with each and every month a different country's food. Our aim is to bring you unusual ingredients and flavours and tempt you to try something a little different. We also love to test our cooking skills with new dishes and exotic ingredients.
